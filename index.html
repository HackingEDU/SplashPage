What


HackingEDU Training Day is a pre-hackathon event full of workshops and hands-on-training. For an entire day, mentors and tech guides will give talks on various technologies and work with students to help them master the tools they will need to take their skills to the next level.

Why


Want to learn a new API, language or framework? Have a specific question or issue? Come to Training Day and learn from the best! Attendees are guaranteed admission to the main Hackathon in October and access to view the talks and recordings, so sign up today!

Where


Paypal Town Hall 2161 N 1st St
San Jose, CA

FAQ

Hacker FAQ | Sponsor FAQ

What should I bring?

A government or student ID, your laptop, and a hunger for learning!

How do I get there?

We will have shuttles available from UC Davis, UC Berkeley and San Francisco. There is also ample parking if you want to drive, and you can also take Caltrain/Public Transportation.Directions to Training Day

How can I get a shuttle to my school?

Send us an email at team@hackingedu.co and get your friends to sign up! Shuttles will be provided to the schools with the largest amount of signups in the SF Bay Area.

Will there be food?

YES! We are providing food & beverages for all attendees at no cost.

How about the full sponsorship package?

We have a comprehensive sponsorship document right here!

I'm interested in custom Sponsorship Packages.

No problem! Contact us at sponsor@hackingedu.co and let us know what you are interested in. We have special options for Startups, Incubators & VCs, and we are always willing to work with companies to create a custom package that works best for you.

Can I still register for a workshop on Training Day?

We only have a couple slots left, so if you are interested, contact us right away at sponsor@hackingedu.co.
